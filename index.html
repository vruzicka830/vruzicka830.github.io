Research

I study operator algebras. I proved (with Chunguang Li and Zhuang Niu) that Villadsen algebras of the first type are singly generated. The paper discussing this result has been submited to the Journal of Noncommutative Geometry.

Biography

I was born in Milwaukee, WI and grew up about 20 miles north of Milwaukee in Cedarburg, WI. I received an undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics, Engineering, and Physics (BS AMEP) from the University of Wisconsin–Madison in 2018. After graduating, I worked as an engineer at National Electrostatics Corporation (NEC) in Middleton, WI where I helped design particle accelerator systems. I left NEC in the fall of 2020 to study math at the University of Wyoming.
