Research Description

I study C*-algebras. I have spent some time on the generator problem (Is every simple separable unital C*-algebra singly generated?) and I am currently looking at the structure of some "noncommutative" Villadsen algebras.

Biography

I was born in Milwaukee, WI and grew up about 20 miles north of Milwaukee in Cedarburg, WI. I received an undergraduate degree in Applied Mathematics, Engineering, and Physics (BS AMEP) from the University of Wisconsin in 2018. After graduating, I worked as an engineer at National Electrostatics Corporation (NEC) in Middleton, WI where I helped design ion accelerator systems. I left NEC in the fall of 2020 to study math at the University of Wyoming.

Aside from doing math, I like to ski, bike, and run with friends.
